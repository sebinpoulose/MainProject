\ul\b COURSE IN HOSPITAL\par
\lang16393\ulnone\b0 Mrs. Aathira P Panicker, 26 year old lady a known case of bronchia asthma presented in Emergency Medicine department with complaints of cough and breathing difficulty for 10 days. Initially she was evaluated at a hospital in Banglore with complaints of left eye bluring of vision. She was managed with eye drops and was improoved. Mantoux was positive. She had sudden onset of hemoptysis and cough with expectoration. She presented to Aster Medicity, Cochin for the same complaints. Sputum culture and sensitivity showed gram positive cocci. Bacterial culture showed predominant growth of staphylococcus aureus (MRSA). In view of worsening symptoms she was initiated on empirical anti tuberculous treatment since 14-02-2019. CT chest showed multiple mediastinal lymphnodes along with peribronchial and perihilar shadows and mucosal irregularity. She was treated with IV antibiotics (Cefaperazone Salbactum and Azithromycin), antiviral (Fluvir), ATT (AKT4), \lang1033 systemic steroid\lang16393  and other supportive measures. Due to finacial constraints, she was referred to another centre for further management.\par
\lang1033 With this background she presented here. Her chest radiograph showed bilateral alveolar infiltrates. She was admitted in Medical ICU under Pulmonary Medicine and treated with IV antibiotics (Meropenem, Linezolid and Doxycycline), antiviral (Fluvir), bronchodilators and other supportive measures. Screening ECHO cardiogram showed good LV function, no RWMA and no RA/ RV dilation. Her baseline blood investigation showed elevated ESR, CRP, PT INR values as well as hyponatremia.\b  \b0 Bronchoscopy was done which showed thick mucopurulent secretions in bilateral bronchial segments; thorough lavage was done and BAL was sent for appropriate microbial investigations. Cardiology consultation was taken and advised to do detailed ECHO. Opthalmology consultation was taken for fundes examination and advices were followed. Serial chest radiograph showed borderline better. Mantoux test was done which was positive.
