Mr Saibu Mathew, 44 year old male, presented with complaints right upper limb varicose vein for past 5 yrs with recent onset pain. On Doppler evaluation he was found to have varicose veins along upper arm. CECT done in adducted and abducted positions of right upper arm showed suggestion of a small spur from the 1\super st\nosupersub  rib with narrowing of the subclavian vein noted at the costoclavicular space. Prominent collaterals noted in upper arm and right side of neck filling the right IJV.\par
