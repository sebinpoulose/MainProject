\ul\b Course in the hospital and Discussion:\ulnone\b0\par

His routine blood investigations showed hemoglobin value of 14.7 g/dl, elevated ESR and mild hypokalemia and elevated CEA value of 11.85. He was kept NPO and  continuous RT aspiration was done. Nature of his illness and need for further evaluation has been explained in detail to patient relatives. Hence he is being discharged at request with an advice to continue further management elsewhere.\par
