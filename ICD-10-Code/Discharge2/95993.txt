HOPI\lang1033\b0 :\lang3081\par

He was initially taken to a local hospital, intubated and ventilated there and later referred here for further management.\par

\pard CT (15/02/2019)\lang1033\b0 :  \f4 Massive intraparenchymal haemorrhage noted in left gangliocapsular region measuring 5.2 x 3.1 x 3.8 cm ( AP x ML x CC ) with extension to left thalamus and midbrain.Mass effect noted in the form of effacement of adjacent sulci and 3 rd ventricle.
